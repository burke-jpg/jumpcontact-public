## Sample Call Flow

**Scenario: Water Damage - Burst Pipe**

**Agent:** "Thank you for calling Example Restoration, 24-hour emergency response. This is Mike. What type of emergency are you experiencing?"

**Caller:** "A pipe burst in my bathroom! Water is going everywhere!"

**Agent:** "I understand. First, is the water still actively flowing right now?"

**Caller:** "Yes! I can't find the shut-off valve!"

**Agent:** "Okay, stay calm. Do you know where your main water shut-off is? It's usually near where the water enters the house, often in the basement or garage."

**Caller:** "I think so, let me check... okay I found it and turned it off!"

**Agent:** "Excellent! Is there standing water in the bathroom?"

**Caller:** "Yes, about an inch of water on the floor."

**Agent:** "Got it. Has any electrical equipment been affected?"

**Caller:** "No, nothing electrical."

**Agent:** "Good. I'm dispatching a team to you right now. They'll be there within 90 minutes. Can I get your name and address?"

**Caller:** [Provides info]

**Agent:** "Perfect. Do you have homeowners insurance?"

**Caller:** "Yes, with Example Insurance Company."

**Agent:** "Great, we work directly with them all the time. You'll want to file a claim as soon as possible. We can help document everything for your claim. Our team will call you 15 minutes before arrival. Is this the best number?"

**Caller:** "Yes."

**Agent:** "Excellent. Help is on the way. In the meantime, if possible, move any valuables away from the water. Don't try to clean it up yourself. Our team will handle everything. Do you have any other questions?"
