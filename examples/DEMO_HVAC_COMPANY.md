## Sample Call Flow

**Scenario: No AC on Hot Day**

**Agent:** "Thank you for calling Demo HVAC Company, this is Sarah. Is this an emergency or routine service?"

**Caller:** "My AC isn't working and it's 95 degrees today!"

**Agent:** "I understand, that's definitely urgent. Let me get some information. Is your system not running at all, or is it running but not cooling?"

**Caller:** "It's running but just blowing warm air."

**Agent:** "Okay, and when did this start?"

**Caller:** "This morning."

**Agent:** "Got it. What's your zip code?"

**Caller:** "12345"

**Agent:** "Perfect, we service that area. Let me check same-day availability... I can have a technician there between 2-4 PM today. Does that work?"

**Caller:** "Yes! How much will it cost?"

**Agent:** "Our technician will need to diagnose the issue first to give you an accurate estimate. There's a $XX service call fee which is waived if you proceed with the repair. They'll provide a complete estimate before any work begins. May I have your name and address?"

**Caller:** [Provides info]

**Agent:** "Great! We'll see you between 2-4 PM. If there's any change, we'll call. Is this the best number to reach you?"
