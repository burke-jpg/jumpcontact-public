### **6-Block Modular Architecture**

Each ticket consists of 6 independent, swappable blocks:

<div align="center">

| Block | Purpose | Update Frequency |
|:------|:--------|:-----------------|
| **1. Header** | Business branding & logo | Rarely (rebrands only) |
| **2. Status Bar** | Live time, weather, office status | Never (auto-updates) |
| **3. Business Details** | Contact info with click-to-copy | Rarely (contact changes) |
| **4. Call Scripts** | Agent guidance & call flow | Occasionally (optimization) |
| **5. Forms** | GHL form integration | As needed (field changes) |
| **6. Warnings** | Critical protocols & compliance | Rarely (policy changes) |
